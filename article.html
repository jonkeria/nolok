What's exciting at Foy's Lake Basecamp in Chittagong?

Saturday, Dec. 23, 16:15h
By Admin

Chittagong is the favorite destination of many because it has both mountains and sea. Along with nature and heritage sightings, adventure seekers can keep Foy's Lake Basecamp on their list. What is there? Fahim Al Samad reports

Surrounded by trees. Among them is a lake of calm blue water on the mountain. You can see wild deer or rabbits while taking a boat on that lake. Along with the natural beauty, you can enjoy all the thrilling rides. Foy's Lake is the name of this recreation center built on 336 acres of hilly area of ​​Chittagong city.

'Basecamp' was launched at Foy's Lake this year. This joint venture of Concord Group of Companies and Basecamp Adventures Limited has various activities to increase physical and mental skills. A group activity 'Tree Top Activity' centered around a few trees. Five obstacle 'on ground activities' for thrill seekers. 'Team building game' for leadership development. There are also opportunities for kayaking in the lake water. Archery to train the bow and arrow. You can go from one side of the lake to the other side by 'water zip line'. There are also giant swings and hammocks for swinging.

You can come to Chittagong from anywhere in the country by CNG-powered auto-rickshaw or car and go to Foy's Lake. Basecamp activities can also be done individually here, but day-long activities and overnight packages are more popular.

Full day at basecamp

The path to the basecamp along the lake. Reception and restaurant at the entrance. You can get some refreshment here to get rid of travel fatigue. After that, the mountain road is divided into two parts. If you follow the straight path, you will reach 'Camping Hill'. And going up to the right is 'Activity Hill'. This hill is decorated with several types of exciting activities. At the beginning there is the 'Zip Line'. But do these activities at the end. From there, you will see tree top activities and on ground activities. In these two exercises, physical skills as well as mental skills should be introduced. Another exciting addition is the giant swing. It is several times longer than a normal swing. You will be pulled off the ground at least 10 feet and released. You will enjoy the beauty of Chittagong city from the top of the hill while swinging.

Now let's go to the zip line, one of the attractions of the camp. Hang on the wire and move from side to side over the water of the lake. Children's activities next to Camp Hill for the little ones. After all the activities and lunch, you can spend a nice afternoon lying in the giant hammock. Or you can end the day by kayaking on the lake. The whole day will cost 3 thousand rupees per person. With food from morning to evening.

What to do at night

The true beauty of Basecamp emerges at night. A thrilling atmosphere is created by the call of crickets and the barking of foxes. In the evening, the campfire was lit on the shore of the lake. There are groups of people sitting there singing. If you want, you can spend the whole night sitting here. Or you can go back to the tent and rest. If you want light trekking in moonlight you can go to 'Into the Wild' hill. A winding path of about three kilometers through the forest. You can forget all the tiredness of the day and walk along this path surrounded by nature in the moonlight. If you add camping with all the activities of the day, it will cost 7 thousand rupees per person. All meals will be available till next morning. Keeping in mind the safety of the tourists, there are a few skilled instructors present during each thrilling activity. Basecamp site manager Nazmul Waheed said there are two levels of security for the safety of tourists. All arrangements are made here to provide maximum security and service to the tourists.
